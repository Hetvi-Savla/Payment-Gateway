THIS IS THE FACE OF CLEAN WATER

When it comes down to it, we’re all about people, just like our name says. Stories of people’s lives changed by safe water and sanitation inspire us to keep at it, day after day. Your generosity has changed millions of lives around the world. Here are some of their stories.

A Dream of Safe Water

"It was like a dream to us," says Lilian. She says the people in her community never thought they would have safe water."When people came to tell us about the water project we didn’t believe it would happen – until we saw people actually constructing the water system," Lilian says. "Now we have water a few meters from our houses!"

Water Brings a New Hope

THIS IS A WATER CHARITY THAT WORKS


We believe in a world where everyone has safe drinking water, forever.


We partner with local governments, businesses, and individuals, providing the support they need to bring clean water and sanitation systems to their own communities. Then we ensure they have the resources they need to maintain those systems now and in the future. The local government provides the structure.The local shop owner provides the materials. The local entrepreneur makes repairs.


And the local family gets clean water for a lifetime.
